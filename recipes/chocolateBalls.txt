name
Chocolate Coconut Balls

ingredients
200 gram biscuits
10 tablespoon sugar
5 tablespoon cocoa
7 tablespoon milk
1 teaspoon vanille extract
100 gram margarine

instructions
Wrap the biscuits in a bag and break them into rice-like crumbs, using a rolling pin. 
Place the crumbs in a bowl and whisk together with sugar and cocoa.  
Add milk, vanille extract and margarine and beat until combined.
Using full teaspoons, make small size balls from the mixture and decorate with coconut powder or Colored sugar. 
Place in small paper cups and refrigirate 3 hours before serving.
